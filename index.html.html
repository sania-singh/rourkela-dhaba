About Rourkela Dhaba

Rourkela Dhaba is more than just a restaurant—it's an experience. Established with a passion for authentic Indian flavors, we bring the true taste of traditional roadside dhabas to the heart of Amritsar.

Our chefs use time-honored recipes and cooking techniques passed down through generations. We source our spices directly from local growers and prepare every dish with the same care and attention as a home-cooked meal.

Located conveniently near the Toll Plaza on G.T. Road, we pride ourselves on our warm hospitality, generous portions, and commitment to serving delicious food 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Whether you're craving buttery parathas, rich curries, or sizzling tandoori dishes, Rourkela Dhaba is your destination for authentic Indian flavors.

From families enjoying weekend meals to travelers stopping by at odd hours, we welcome everyone to experience the true essence of Indian dhaba culture.
